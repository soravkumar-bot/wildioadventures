FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all. We have trips graded from beginner to expert. Just bring your spirit of adventure.

What's included in the trip price?

All guides, permits, camping gear, and most meals are included. International flights are separate.

Can I join solo?

Absolutely. Most of our explorers join as solo travelers and leave as lifelong friends.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 30 days before departure. 50% refund up to 14 days. No refund within 14 days.
