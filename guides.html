Outdoor Safety Tips

Stay safe so you can keep exploring for years to come.

📡

Tell Someone Your Plan

Always share your itinerary and expected return time with a trusted contact.

🩹

Carry a First Aid Kit

Know the basics: blister care, wound cleaning, and how to handle altitude sickness.

🔦

📱

Offline Navigation

Gear Up

Packing Essentials

The right gear makes all the difference in the wild.

🎒 The Right Backpack

Choose a pack with proper hip support. For day hikes 20–30L, for multiday trips 50–70L.

🧥 Layer System

Base layer (moisture wicking), mid layer (insulation), outer layer (wind/waterproof).

🍫 Nutrition on the Trail

High-calorie, lightweight foods. Nuts, energy bars, dried fruit, and instant meals.
